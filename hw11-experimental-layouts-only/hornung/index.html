Green Bean Ravioli

Ingredients

  • 1 20 oz package of cheese filled ravioli
  • 1 8 oz package of fresh green beans
  • 1/2 cup of chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh sage
  • 1/2 cup of shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ravioli and green beans in boiling water and cover in a Dutch oven 4-5 minutes or until the green beans are crisp-tender. Then drain.
  2. Heat pecans in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat for 2-3 minutes or until toasted and fragranted. Stir often. Remove from skillet when done.
  3. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add garlic and saute fo 5-7 minutes or until garlic is caramel color and butter begins to brown. Remove from heat.
  5. Stir in sage, ravioli, green beans, and pecans.
  6. Sprinkle with cheese.
